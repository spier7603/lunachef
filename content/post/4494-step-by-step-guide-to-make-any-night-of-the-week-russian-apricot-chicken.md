##### The ingredients needed to make Russian Apricot Chicken:

1. Prepare 1 can apricot preserves
1. Take 1 envelope Lipton onion soup mix
1. Make ready 1 packages Russian dressing
1. Make ready 1 packages chicken breasts




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Russian Apricot Chicken:

1. Preheat oven to 350
1. In a glass baking dish, lay chicken breasts in a row.
1. Spoon out apricot preserves over chicken. Pour Russian dressing over that. Sprinkle soup mix over that.
1. Add 1/4 cup of water
1. Cook, uncovered, for an hour. Drizzle sauce over chicken when finished. Serve with salad and green beans.
