##### The ingredients needed to make Ice Milk Butterfly pea:

1. Take 1 cup butterfly pea
1. Take 100 ml warm milk
1. Prepare 75 ml condensed milk
1. Get 75 ml hot water
1. Take 22 oz ice

##### Instructions to make Ice Milk Butterfly pea:

1. To separate the butterfly pea’s pollen by pull the green at the bottom or you can use dried butterfly pea version you have at home.
1. Boil 75 ml of water in a pot.add the butterfly pea until the water get colored. Drain and leave a side to cool down.
1. Warm the milk in a pot and add the condensed milk and stir well for 30 seconds and leave it a side to cool down.
1. Prepare the ice in a glass, add 3/4 of milk and butterfly pea water for the rest.
