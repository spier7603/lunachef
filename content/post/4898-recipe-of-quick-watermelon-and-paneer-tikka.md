1. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala powder
1. Make ready 1 tsp fennel seed powder
1. Take 1 tbsp cream
1. Take 2 tsp salt

1. Make ready 1 tsp chopped mint and coriander leaves
1. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
