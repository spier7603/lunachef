##### The ingredients needed to make Paneer Bhurji with Peas:

1. Take 200 g Paneer
1. Take 2 Onions chopped
1. Make ready 2 Tomatoes chopped
1. Make ready 1 Green chillis chopped
1. Take 1/2 bowl Peas (boiled)
1. Prepare to taste Salt
1. Get 1/4 tsp Red chilli powder
1. Make ready 1/4 tsp Garam masala
1. Make ready  Turmeric (optional)
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Mustard oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Paneer Bhurji with Peas:

1. Chopped onions, tomatoes, green chillis one by one.
1. Take a kadhai heat the oil smoking point and simmer the flame.Add chopped onions and cook for 1 mint.
1. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilis and cook for 1 mint.
1. Add all dry spices and mix well. Add boiled peas and crushed paneer and cook again. Ready your paneer Bhurji and serve with roti or Parantha.
